Solution

The Valorem health platform provides a comprehensive suite of software tools to capture, analyse, and visualise patient experience data, such as patient preferences and patient-reported outcomes.

Our applications are:

  • Web-based, mobile-friendly, and designed to be intuitive and easy to use.
  • secure and compliant with industry standards (HIPAA, FDA CFR Part 11, GDPR, etc.).
  • Scalable and flexible, to easily integrate with existing systems and processes.

Use cases

#1 Capture patient experiences in clinical trial

Understanding what matters to patients is important at any stage of the medical product lifecycle. It can inform, for example, product development, trial design, regulatory and reimbursement decisions. Methods such as multi-dimensional thresholding and swing weighting allow us to assess patient preferences for benefit-risk and health technology assessment in clinical trials.

#2 Value assessment

Valuing health outcomes (EQ-5D, SF-6D, etc.) is an essential component in health technology assessment. In addition to standard valuation methods, such as time trade-off, we offer Online elicitation of Personal Utility Functions (OPUF), which allows capturing individual (patient) preferences and use them even for valuing benefits of treatments for rare diseases.

#3 Patient engagement

We offer a range of tools to engage patients in research or policy decisions. Our survey platform can be used, for example, to support patient advisory board discussion with real-time capture and visualisation of patient preferences and priorities.

Research

If you have an idea for a research project, or if you would like to use our software for your research, please do get in touch – we offer significant discounts for academics and are keen to collaborate on interesting projects.

About us

Valorem health was founded by Dr Paul Schneider and Dr Tommi Tervonen in 2023. During our previous roles in consultancy and academia, we led dozens of scientific patient preference studies to support development and commercialization of innovative therapies.

We saw that the lack of suitable software was a major barrier to the wider availability and use of patient experience data – in rare diseases and in clinical trials, it even often meant that the patient perspective was omitted completely.

Our mission is to make it easy for organisations to capture, analyse, and visualise patient experience data at any stage of the medical product lifecycle.
