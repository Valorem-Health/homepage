Use cases

#1 Capture patient experiences in (early) clinical trial

Understanding what matters to patient is important at any stage of the medical product lifecycle. It can inform, for example, product development, trial design, or regulatory decisions. Methods such as Multi-dimensional Thresholding (MDT), or Swing Weighting allow us to assess patient preferences for benefit-risk assessment even in early clinical trials.

#2 Value assessment

Valuing health outcomes (EQ-5D, SF-6D, etc.) is an essential component in health technology assessment. In addition to standard valuation methods, such as time trade-off, we offer Online elicitation of Personal Utility Functions (OPUF), which allows capturing (patient) preferences with small sample sizes and even on the level of the individual patient.

#3 Patient engagement

We offer a range of tools to engage patients in research or policy decisions. Our survey platform can be used, for example, to support patient advisory board discussion with real-time capture and visualisation of patient preferences and priorities.

About us

The patient's perspective should be at the center of decision making in clinical care, health policy, and medical product development. For this, relevant patient preference information needs to be much more widely available. It then just doesn't make sense that collecting patient preference studies is so difficult, costly, and time consuming...

Our mission is to make it easy for organisations to capture, analyse, and visualise patient preferences at any stage of the medical product lifecycle.
